About Escape Raid

Play Escape Raid now and take on the role of a sheriff hunted by relentless foes in a heart-pounding escape. Dodge danger, avoid obstacles, stay on the move, and see how long you can survive this endless pursuit.

What is Escape Raid

Escape Raid is a fast-paced car driving game where the main objective is to stay ahead of the enemies chasing you, trying to survive as long as possible without crashing or slowing down enough to get caught. Set in the post-apocalyptic wasteland, you drive your car, speeding down a dangerous road, with enemies on your tail relentlessly.

There’s no place for mistakes—one wrong move and the game ends in a flash. So, sharpen your reflexes and unleash your best driving skills to dodge hazards, go the distance, and rack up points and rewards.

Developer: AzGames.io

Core Gameplay Highlights

Fast-paced car chase and escape, with constant enemies right behind you.

The goal is to race across an infinite, deserted world and last as long as possible without being caught or taken down; no finish line, just survival.

Various car classes with 90 unlockable models, each with its own performance stats.

Multiple types of weapons to equip.

Chaotic, post-apocalyptic wasteland full of danger.

How to Play Escape Raid Game

The chase starts as soon as you drop in. Race at your full speed to escape the enemy pursuit, aiming to survive as long as possible while evading surrounding dangers.

Drive skillfully to dodge dangers

There’s no time pressure in the Escape Raid Game, yet the challenges come from whether you can survive the high-speed chase in a world filled with dangers, obstacles, and many hazards. Stay sharp and steer the vehicle with precision to stay safe and keep the wheels turning to survive.

This game was inspired by Escape Road game but adds a totally new twist! Instead of taking the role of a bank robber trying to escape from the police, now, players will become a police and escaping from the enemies.

Run as far as possible and fire at enemies

Stay alive and keep moving for as long as you can. Fire back or strike when enemies close in; survival depends on your driving skills, quick reflexes, and precision.

Upgrade the vehicle

You will earn points and rewards based on your performance. Spend your rewards to unlock upgraded cars and weapons, each boosting your power and performance for a stronger run next time.

Game controls

  • WASD or the arrow keys: Steer the car.
  • The spacebar or left mouse click: Attack
  • Q: Switch weapons
  • C: Change camera view
  • E: Lock the current weapon
